About Us

CKT Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Kumaragiri was established in 1999 with 300 students and 20 teachers for the benefit of the students of kovilpatti and its neighborhood. CKT Matric Hr.Sec. School functions under the auspices of Chennai Kammavar Trust. The school was recognized by the Government of Tamilnadu in 2000 and has classes upto Std-XII.

Our School is situated in a sprawling campus. This 30 acre land is abutting Ettaiyapuram- Kovilpatti road away from the hustle and bustle of the crowded atmosphere. The school has well- ventilated, spacious classrooms furnished with ample furniture. The school has vast playgrounds, beautiful gardens and kids playground. Eminence through Excellence is the motto of our school. Our Students are trained to excel in every aspect and thereby attain to eminence. We believe a wholesome pursuit of a wholesome education leads to excellence, which in turn paves the way for eminence.

Our Facilities

FOOD

Our school provides hygienic food in Lunch and Milk & Biscuits during short breaks. No need of carrying water bottles Mineral Water is available in the campus all the time.

TUTOR

For K.G., lessons are taught through Montessori and phonic method Audio Visual teaching with trained and well qualified teachers to teach all the classes from KG to XII std.

CASH AWARD

Every year we are honouring the achievers of X and XII std students with Cash Awards and Memorial Awards.


TRANSPORT FACILITIES

The school provides transport facility for the students and charges are collected according to the distance. The school bus will stop only at fixed points. Students should be present at the pickup points in time and bus will not await the arrival of students who are late.




LABORATORY

School provides separate labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Computer Science with all facilities, Albums, Model, Charts, stuffed and preserved specimens and other aids form part of the Lab. This year the students will enjoy learning language through laboratory. A full fledged English lab is available for the purpose. Smart Class lab is functioning from the year 2021.

LIBRARY

The school library has a wonderful collection of more than 8000 books on various subjects. One period is allotted every week, so that, every student gets an opportunity to visit the library regularly. This is to develop the habit of reading in school days and pupil should maintain a record of the extra reading done by them in a separate note book.


EXTRA CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Music, Dance, Yoga, Karate, Chess, Carrom, Skatting and Silambam are taught inside the campus by experts from the respective fields.










SPORTS & GAMES

The school offers competitive facilities for playing games like Cricket, Basket Ball, Volley Ball, Foot Ball, Badminton, throw ball, Tennis and kho-kho. The students are also trained in indoor games like table tennis, Shuttle Badminton, Carrom and chess.






HOSTEL FAILITIES

Our school provides separate Hostel facilities for boys from VI std to XII. Spacious rooms with cot, hot water during winter season, delicious food, special care and attention for the hostel students in their studies and health are the attractive features of our school. Especially for X and XII standard students, our Management provides free food and accommodation from January onwards to attend special coaching classes.
